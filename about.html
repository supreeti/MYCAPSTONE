EDULEARN CONFERENCE 2023

About Conference: Welcome to the official website International Conference on Education and New Learning Technologies (EDULEARN). The conference provides a platform for professionals involved in Education and New Learning Technologies to exchange knowledge and gain an insight into the state of the art in the current technology, techniques and solutions in Education and New Learning Technologies as they have been developed and applied in different countries. Participants include a wide variety of stakeholders from research and academia, to industrial sectors as well as government organizations.
